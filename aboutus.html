About Us

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) is a prominent professional organization in India with a mission to advance the fields of electronics, telecommunications and information technology. Headquartered in New Delhi, it is dedicated to advancing the fields of electronics, telecommunications and information technology. IETE offers various categories of membership, including Student, Associate, Member and Fellow, providing access to valuable resources and networking opportunities. Recognized by the Indian government as a scientific and industrial research organization, IETE continues to foster research, development and education in the dynamic fields of electronics and telecommunications, making significant contributions to technological advancements in the country.
